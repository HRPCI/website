Terms & Conditions for Awardees

By accepting an award or recognition from Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI), the recipient agrees to follow the following terms and ethical responsibilities.

General Terms

  • Awards are honorary acknowledgements only.
  • They do not provide any legal, academic or government authority.
  • Awardees must represent the recognition ethically.

Usage Terms

  • Awards must not be misused for fraud or false claims.
  • No award should be presented as Government, University or UN statutory recognition.
  • Honorary Doctorate recognition must be clearly represented as “Honorary”.

Public Representation

  • Awardees may publicly acknowledge their award responsibly.
  • Awardees allow HRPCI to publish their names and photos for record and transparency.

No Guarantee or Legal Rights

  • No employment, legal benefit or privilege is attached to awards.
  • Participation is voluntary and non-refundable unless officially stated.

Withdrawal Policy

  • Award may be cancelled if false information is discovered.
  • Misuse for illegal or unethical purposes will result in immediate withdrawal.
  • HRPCI decision is final and binding.
Legal Disclaimer:
All recognitions are honorary and social acknowledgements. By accepting the award, the recipient agrees to these Terms & Conditions without dispute.
