About Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI)

Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI) is a national-level social organization established to promote education, social awareness, ethical leadership, and human dignity. The Council works to identify, support, and recognize individuals who make meaningful contributions towards social development and nation-building.

HRPCI is a registered Trust under the Indian Trust Act, Registration No. 1142503987. The organization operates in compliance with applicable Indian laws and follows principles of transparency, accountability, and social responsibility.

HRPCI is registered on the NGO Darpan portal of NITI Aayog, Government of India, with Unique ID OR/2025/0837673, and is mapped with the Ministry of Social Justice (NGO ID: OR/00049686).

The organization is also listed with the United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs (UNDESA), reflecting its alignment with international development goals and global human rights values.

Our Vision

To build an aware, educated, and inclusive society where human rights, dignity, and social responsibility are respected and practiced by all.

Our Mission

  • To promote awareness of human rights and constitutional values
  • To encourage education, ethical leadership, and social responsibility
  • To identify and recognize individuals contributing positively to society
  • To organize seminars, workshops, and awareness programs
  • To inspire youth and citizens towards nation-building initiatives

Our Goals & Objectives

  • Organizing national and regional seminars on social and educational issues
  • Recognizing deserving individuals through awards and honorary recognitions
  • Promoting volunteerism and community participation
  • Supporting initiatives aligned with national development priorities
  • Working in alignment with United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Our Activities

Human Rights Protection Council, India regularly organizes seminars, awareness programs, social campaigns, and public discussions. The Council actively works to identify individuals from diverse fields such as education, social service, healthcare, environmental protection, public welfare, and humanitarian work, and honors them through awards and recognitions for their outstanding contributions.

National & International Outlook

HRPCI functions at a national level and collaborates with socially responsible individuals and institutions. Its activities are inspired by constitutional values, human rights principles, and internationally accepted development frameworks.

Legal & Ethical Disclaimer

Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI) is an independent social organization. It is not a government body and does not exercise any statutory, judicial, or law enforcement authority. All activities are conducted within the framework of Indian law and ethical standards.
