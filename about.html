About Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI)

Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI) is a national-level social organization committed to promoting human dignity, education, social awareness, and constitutional values across India. The organization works with the vision of creating an informed, responsible, and empowered society.

HRPCI actively engages in social awareness programs, educational initiatives, public seminars, community outreach, and recognition of individuals who contribute meaningfully to society. The Council believes that awareness, education, and ethical leadership are the foundations of a just nation.

Our Vision

To build a society where human rights, education, and social responsibility are respected, protected, and practiced by every citizen.

Our Mission

  • To promote awareness of human rights and constitutional values
  • To support education, social reform, and community development
  • To encourage ethical leadership and social responsibility
  • To recognize individuals and organizations contributing to nation-building
  • To organize seminars, workshops, and public awareness programs

What We Do

HRPCI conducts social awareness campaigns, educational seminars, volunteer programs, and public discussions on important social issues. The Council also recognizes outstanding individuals through awards and honorary recognitions for their contribution to education, social service, public welfare, and human values.

Our Values

  • Integrity and transparency
  • Respect for human dignity
  • Social responsibility
  • National unity and harmony
  • Commitment to education and awareness

Legal & Ethical Note

Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI) is a social organization and works independently for awareness, education, and social welfare. HRPCI is not a government body and does not claim any statutory or judicial authority. All activities are conducted within the framework of Indian law and ethical standards.
