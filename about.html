About Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI)

The Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI) is a nationally registered trust working for the promotion and protection of human rights, social justice, education, peace, and humanitarian values across India and beyond. The organization is committed to empowering individuals, supporting underprivileged communities, and recognizing outstanding contributions made toward the betterment of society.

HRPCI believes that every human being deserves dignity, equality, opportunity, and respect regardless of background, caste, religion, gender, or social status. Through awareness programs, educational initiatives, legal assistance support, social campaigns, and recognition of social contributors, HRPCI strives to build a just, inclusive, and compassionate society.

Legal Status & Recognition

  • Registered under the Indian Trust Act (Reg. No. 1142503987)
  • Registered on NGO Darpan (Government of India)
  • NGO Darpan Unique ID: OR/2025/0837673
  • Mapped with the Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment
  • NGO ID: OR/00049686
  • Listed with UNDESA (United Nations Department of Economic and Social Affairs)

Our Core Areas of Work

  • Promotion and protection of human rights
  • Social justice and public welfare initiatives
  • Educational development and awareness programs
  • Women, child, and senior citizen rights advocacy
  • Legal awareness and victim support programs
  • Environmental awareness and sustainable development
  • Peace-building and humanitarian service initiatives

Recognition & Awards

HRPCI actively identifies and honors individuals and organizations who demonstrate exceptional dedication to social service, humanitarian work, education, leadership, innovation, and nation-building. Through award ceremonies, seminars, conferences, and social programs, HRPCI provides platforms to recognize deserving contributors whose efforts positively impact society.

Our Mission

To promote human dignity, social justice, education, peace, and equality by empowering individuals, protecting human rights, and recognizing contributions toward nation-building and humanitarian service.

Our Vision

To build a society where every individual enjoys fundamental rights, social respect, equal opportunity, and access to justice, education, and sustainable development.

Our Goals

  • To protect and promote human rights at all levels
  • To support education, awareness, and social development
  • To empower marginalized and vulnerable communities
  • To recognize and honor social contributors
  • To promote peace, unity, and ethical leadership
  • To operate in compliance with national and international human rights standards

Our Activities

Human Rights Protection Council, India regularly organizes seminars, awareness programs, social campaigns, and public discussions. The Council actively works to identify individuals from diverse fields such as education, social service, healthcare, environmental protection, public welfare, and humanitarian work, and honors them through awards and recognitions for their outstanding contributions.

What We Do

HRPCI conducts social awareness campaigns, educational seminars, volunteer programs, and public discussions on important social issues. The Council also recognizes outstanding individuals through awards and honorary recognitions for their contribution to education, social service, public welfare, and human values.

Our Values

  • Integrity and transparency
  • Respect for human dignity
  • Social responsibility
  • National unity and harmony
  • Commitment to education and awareness

National & International Outlook

HRPCI functions at a national level and collaborates with socially responsible individuals and institutions. Its activities are inspired by constitutional values, human rights principles, and internationally accepted development frameworks.

Ethical Commitment

HRPCI strictly follows ethical standards, legal compliance, and transparency in all activities. All recognitions, memberships, and honorary titles conferred by HRPCI are purely honorary in nature and based on merit. HRPCI does not grant academic degrees, government authority, or statutory rights through its recognitions.

Legal & Ethical Disclaimer

Human Rights Protection Council, India (HRPCI) is an independent social organization.
